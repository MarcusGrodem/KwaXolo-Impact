# KwaXolo Learn — LLM-Optimized Brief

## 1. Core Problem
Digitally transform 9 rural schools (KwaZulu-Natal) to maximize digital literacy and long-term economic opportunity. KwaXolo is directly responsible for Phase 1 (basic PC literacy) and Phase 6 (entrepreneurship). This platform addresses Phase 6.

---

## 2. Product
**KwaXolo Learn** — AI-powered entrepreneurship learning platform.
- Teacher uses AI agent to generate bilingual (EN + isiZulu) modules
- Students consume modules on shared school PCs, offline
- Admin oversees content quality and analytics

---

## 3. Three Roles
| Role | Device | Internet | Core action |
|---|---|---|---|
| Teacher | PC (1 per teacher) | Intermittent (Msenti Hub / 2 schools) | Generate → edit → publish modules |
| Student | Shared PC (4–6 per machine) | Offline (7/9 schools) | Browse → learn → quiz |
| Admin | PC | Online | Review, flag, analytics |

---

## 4. Architecture
**Online generation → Offline consumption**
- Generate at Msenti Hub or 2 connected schools
- Distribute via USB / periodic sync
- Consume offline at all 9 schools

MVP shows online flow only. Offline sync explained in slides.

---

## 5. AI Agent
- Template-driven (25 templates across 6 categories + custom)
- No freeform chat — teacher selects template + adds local context
- Output: full bilingual module (EN + isiZulu) with objectives, content, exercise, quiz, take-home challenge
- Teacher edits inline before publishing (isiZulu QA by native-speaker teacher)

---

## 6. Pedagogical Model: Build–Practice–Present
(From Educate! — 105% income increase in RCT evaluations)
- **Build:** Theory + local example (10 min)
- **Practice:** Group exercise, shared PC (15 min)
- **Present:** Take-home challenge, no computer needed

---

## 7. Local Context
- **Msenti Hub** (Victor Jaca) — incubator, business registration, IT support
- **SEDA Port Shepstone** — free government business registration
- **Payment:** cash-dominant, MTN Mobile Money growing
- **Commerce:** spaza shops, WhatsApp ordering, Facebook Marketplace
- **Existing ventures:** Hlobisile Pearl Studios, 1LT Bakery, Inkify

---

## 8. Growth Sectors (KZN)
IT Services 12.9%, E-Commerce 8.8%, Agri-Tech 7.6%, Food/Artisan 6.1%, Construction 5.2%
Youth unemployment: >60%

---

## 9. Economic Opportunity Logic
- **Chain A:** Skills → formal employment in growth sectors over time
- **Chain B:** Platform directly enables local micro-business creation now (zero capital)

---

## 10. Language
- Full bilingual: EN + isiZulu toggle on every screen
- LLM generates both simultaneously
- Teacher QAs isiZulu translation before publishing

---

Three working flows: teacher generates → student learns → admin reviews
